Grilled Chicken Breast Recipe for Gym Bros

Procedure:

  1. Wash chicken breast
  2. Heat up the pan Sprinkle salt, pepper and piri piri on top of the chicken breast pieces
  3. Flip the chicken breast pieces and apply the seasonings again
  4. Add 1 tbsp of ghee on top of the hot pan
  5. Once the pan is heated, put the chicken pieces inside the pan
  6. Put the gas on medium heat and let one side cook for 5 minutes
  7. After 5 minutes flip the pieces of chicken and let the other side cook for another five minutes
  8. ENJOY THE MEAL :)
